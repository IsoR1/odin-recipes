Pizza

Description

A Neapolitan Pizza with fresh buffalo Mozzarella

Ingredients

  • 00 Flour
  • San Marzano Tomatoes
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil
  • Love!
  1. Mix water, salt, and flour until it forms a dough
  2. Knead the dough until smooth
  3. Cover and rest
  4. Preheat oven
  5. Make pizza balls out of dough
  6. Combine Tomatoes, Salt, Basil, and Olive Oil into sauce
  7. Assemble pizza
  8. Bake
  9. Enjoy!
